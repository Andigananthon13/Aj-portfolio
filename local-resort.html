Welcome to Rain and Angel Resort

Local Resort Website

This project showcases the website designed for Rain&Angel. The design focuses on providing a luxurious and relaxing online experience that reflects the resort's ambiance.

Design Choices

The website features a clean layout with a focus on large, high-quality images to highlight the beauty of the resort. The color scheme uses calming colors such as blues and greens to evoke a sense of tranquility.

Functionalities

  • Online booking
  • Virtual view for cottages
  • Interactive map of the resort facilities
  • Customer reviews and ratings
